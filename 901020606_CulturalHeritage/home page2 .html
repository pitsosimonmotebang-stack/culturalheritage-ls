Welcome to the Heart of the Mountain Kingdom - LESOTHO

Nestled high in the rugged peaks of Southern Africa, Lesotho is a land where tradition, beauty, and warmth meet. Known as the Kingdom in the Sky, it is celebrated fot its majestic mountains, vibrant culture, and the enduring spirit of Basotho people. This space is designed to showcase the rich heritage, crafts, and hospitality of Lesotho from the iconic Basotho blanket and the Mokorotlo Hat to the melodies, dances and values that unite its people. whether you are a visitor, a learner, or a proud Mosotho, we welcome you to discover, connect, and celebrate the unique story of our nation.
